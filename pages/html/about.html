Conduit educational platform

What Conduit platform is?

From the french conduit means behavior. And there was a special journal in some educational institutions, called conduit, for recording students' misdemeanors. With the development of society conduits turned into ordinary school diaries. So this platform is some kind of electronic diary for different kinds of educational organizations.

Our main goal is to make education open and available for everybody by connecting organizators of different educational events with potential participants. We are welcome you to join us and recieve useful experience.

How I can use Conduit platform?

There are several strategies that can be chosen by you for using this platform:

  • I organize

    This strategy is recomended for oranizations or individual persons, who want to connect two parts of education: teachers and students, and want to do this convenient.

    We know how it is hard to manage contacts, groups, schedules, billings, and many other things in EXCEL tables. So we offer you opportunities of conduit to make bureaucracy work easier.

    Our platform has the next stucture: each event (summer camp, club of interests, ...) refers to some period of time, and we assign period as basic item. For each period users will need to make new applications for participation (due to many reasons: students become elder and change classes, or even become teachers; structures reorganizations; ...). In period users can be divided in groups of different types: INFORMATION, ELIMINATION, STUDY; with their own roles, privacy and rules. Group is the second structure item, which includes participants (teachers, students) and study events (topics, olympiads, games, ...). The last ones can be customized in different ways: topics can contains problems, slides, vieos, attachmentns; olympiads can be short or long, writing or oral; games from list implemented ones, rated or unrated, personal or in team. And everything is reinforced with standard requirements as checking attendance, performance assessment, statistics and so on.

  • I teache

    This strategy is recomended to someone, who wants to be directly involoved in study process and spend time telling interesting, useful things; developing skills and abilities; checking homeworks and wants to do this convenient.

    From the first hands we know how it is painful to enter results in a table when they are on many sheets of paper and change during the assessment; we know that students often lose their papers with problems or that you want to show some material but it is too expensive to print it for each student. So we tried to solve such difficulties in some ways on conduit platform and suggest that you appreciate it.

    In groups you can arrange next activities:

    • Sheet is collection of problems that should be solved in bounded period of time. At the same time it can be used for topics which are explained in class room or for olympiads which also can be of different types (short / long, oral / writing, rated / unrated, ...). Customization parameters will give you the opportunities to make everything that you want: duration, content, media content, privacy, rating, attempt limits and so on.
